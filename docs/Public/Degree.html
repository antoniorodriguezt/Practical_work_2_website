However, decision making is also crucial for business analytics career since this is based in marketing, Big Data and predictive analytics. Computer engineering

Computer engineering is a path of engineering careers wich includes multiple paths related with technology and computing like development of hardware systems and software, design… This facets integrate some principles of electrical engineering and computer science to create computer hardware and software systems. Also, this areas encompass sectors such as electromagnetism, artificial intelligence (AI), robotics, computer networks, computer architechture or operating systems. Computer engineer also involves some facets of creation of computer components such as microcoltrollers, microprocessors, development of personal computer, supercomputers or design of electrical circuits based in robotics. From software sector, you can adquire knoweldge about programing to develop programs, apps, websites… based in languages such as C++, HTML, JavaScript among others.

Business analytics

Business analytics, is concerned with the development and application of quantitative approaches to support marginal decision making. It consists in analyse data and build statistical models. Business analytics bachelor is based in the fact of understanding the mainfold contexts of marginal decision-making and combining it with a Deep knoweldge of data analytics techniques involving stadistics and Big Data. This bachelor offers the opportunity of learning skills such as predictive analytics and machine learning, business acumen or problema-solving and decision-making.

Subjets

Year 1:

Basics of Computer Engineering, Economy, Business, Law, Government, University Studies, Critical Thinking, and Skills. Introduction to Data Science, Big Data, and Technology for Business. Mathematics and Engineering courses including Programming, Electronics, and Technology.

Year 2:

Microeconomics, Statistics, and Probability. Humanities: History of Science, Anthropology, Education for Social Responsibility. Data Governance, Big Data, Databases, and Analytics. Mathematics, Discrete Math, and Programming.

Year 3:

Advanced Data Modeling, Visualization, Machine Learning, Big Data Technologies, and Decision Support Systems. Business courses like Marketing, Tax System, Finance, and Technological Entrepreneurship. Cloud Computing and Data Analytics, with Computer Science subjects like Algorithms and System Architecture.

Year 4:

Data and Sustainability, Natural Language Processing, People Analytics, and Financial Analytics. Ethics in the Digital Era and Complementary Training. Computer Engineering topics: Software Engineering, Web Development, AI, and Networks.

Year 5:

Datathon, Business Intelligence, Business Strategy, and Marketing Analytics. Final Degree Projects, External Internships, Software Engineering, HCI, Security, and IT Project Management.

Year 6:

Quality Management and High Performance Computing. Final Degree Project.
